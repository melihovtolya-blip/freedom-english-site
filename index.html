Lesson A1-1

Alphabet, Greetings & "to be" (I/You)

By the end of this lesson you can: say the alphabet A–Z, greet and introduce yourself, use I am / You are, and ask/answer "What's your name?" and "How are you?".

Grammar: I / You + be

I am Alex → I'm Alex.
You are Maria → You're Maria.
Questions: Am I…? Are you…?
Short answers: Yes, I am / No, I'm not; Yes, you are / No, you aren't.

Dialogues

A: Hello!
B: Hi!
A: What's your name?
B: I'm Anna. What's your name?
A: I'm Mark. Nice to meet you, Anna.
B: Nice to meet you, too.
A: Good morning! How are you?
B: I'm fine, thanks. And you?
A: I'm good. Thank you.

Exercises

Fill in am / are:
1) I ____ Anna.
2) You ____ Mark.
3) I ____ fine.
4) You ____ a student.
Complete short answers:
1) Are you Anna? — Yes, I _____.
2) Are you tired? — No, I ____ _____.
Put the dialogue in order (1–6):
Hello!
Hi!
What's your name?
I'm Anna. What's your name?
I'm Mark. Nice to meet you, Anna.
Nice to meet you, too.

Homework

  • Напишите мини-диалог (6–8 реплик) с Hello/Hi, What's your name?, Nice to meet you, How are you?, I'm fine, thanks.
  • Сделайте 5 предложений о себе с "I am …" и 5 вопросов с "Are you …?"
  • Выучите 10 фраз вежливости (Hello, Hi, Good morning, Good afternoon, Goodbye, Bye, Please, Thank you, You're welcome, Excuse me).
